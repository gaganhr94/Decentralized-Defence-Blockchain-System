Decentralized Defense Supply Chain

Blockchain based application primarily aimed at maintaining a digital ledger that records all suppliers/vendors/companies at all levels that have provided hardware/software solutions to manufacture military equipment.

About

This application will assist the government in coordinating with public and private stakeholders, including startups, of all sizes. All suppliers/participants of the supply chain have to register in the blockchain application using the company ID’s, wallet addresses, choose a password for access and transactions, and type of company (public, private, large, small scale industry)

The blockchain application is an immutable, secure ledger, which makes it impossible to access or modify confidential data through cyberattacks.

This project aims to reduce the risk of weapon failure. The blockchain smart contracts will also ensure no counterfeit, illegally imported parts and weapons with malicious code by unauthorized parties enter the defence system by keeping track of all the transactions in the supply. The data of all suppliers for all parts used in defence equipment, stored in a decentralized system with quick access will simplify and speeds up the repair and replacement process.
